Daisy Ridley is an English Actress who earned fame from the 2015 mega-budget film ‘Star Wars: The Force Awakens’. She was born on April 10, 1992, in Westminster, United Kingdom. Through her guest appearances with roles in TV shows, she started her acting career.

She got fame in no time because her talent was enough to make her a Hollywood favorite. She starred in the short film ‘Blue Season’ which entered the Sci-Fi London 48-Hour film challenge. In 2016, she became a voice actor for the 2016 animation film.

Starring in ‘Star Wars: The Force Awakens’ became a turning point in her career. Along with Anne Hathaway, she provided her vocals for the album ‘Encore: Movie Partners Sing Broadway’.
