Kylie Jenner, Iggy Azalea, &amp; Other Celebs Who Hid Their Pregnancies

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Having a child is such an exciting adventure. Pregnant women all over the world have something in common due to the fact that they can relate to what pregnancy feels like. Something that normal individuals and celebrities don’t share in common is what it is like to be pregnant as a famous person. Celebrities deal with public scrutiny, judgment, societal pressures, and social media trolls all the time.

RELATED: TLC’s New Show About Teen Pregnancy Is Totally ‘Unexpected’

That said, some celebrities opt-out of public announcements of their pregnancies so that they can skip all of that nonsense. It is totally up to individual celebrities if they want to share personal details about their lives anyway!

10 Mindy Kaling

Mindy Kaling pretty much shocked everyone when she announced that she secretly gave birth to a baby boy in the middle of quarantine! She already had a daughter named Katherine who is now two years old but now she also has a son named Spencer. She chose not to publicize her pregnancy for her own reasons and she did a really good job of hiding it because pretty much nobody knew.

9 Eva Mendes

Eva Mendes and Ryan Gosling have been together for a good chunk of time after meeting on the set of Beyond the Pines. They totally hit it off and right away decided that they wanted to keep the details of their relationship private. No one knew about her first pregnancy until she was seven months along! The second time she was pregnant she was able to keep it totally under wraps the entire time.

8 Alexis Bledel

Everyone’s favorite Gilmore Girls actress, Alexis Bledel, was able to successfully hide her pregnancy. She’s also been very private about her relationship with Vincent Kartheiser. In fact, the two had a secret wedding ceremony! In 2015, she gave birth to their first child but none of her fans even knew that she was pregnant in the first place. One of her co-stars from Gilmore girls accidentally let the new slip… It wasn’t even Alexis who made the reveal.

7 Iggy Azalea

Australian-born rapper and performer Iggy Azalea gave birth to a child that she shares with Playboi Carti but she decided to keep her pregnancy a secret! She revealed that she gave birth to her son months after he was already born.

RELATED: Every Guy Iggy Azalea Has Dated In Chronological Order

The entire time that she was pregnant, she was posting bikini pictures from the past to trick fans into thinking that she wasn’t pregnant the whole time.

6 Kylie Jenner

One of the biggest and most newsworthy headlines of all time would have to be when Kylie Jenner announced that she had given birth to her daughter, Stormi Webster. She kept her pregnancy a secret the entire time and only her closest friends and family members knew about it. Paparazzi did their best to try to expose her but they were not successful. She announced her pregnancy on her own time and it was a huge deal! People in the media were constantly speculating if she was pregnant or not because no one would ever confirm or deny the rumor.

5 Leighton Meester

Leighton Meester likes to keep things very private and personal when it comes to her family. She is married to Adam Brody and the two of them had an extremely private wedding ceremony back in 2014.

RELATED: Here What Gossip Girl Star Leighton Meester Is Up To Now

Due to the fact that they take their privacy so seriously, keeping her pregnancy a secret was at the top of their priority list. At this point in time, they haven’t released any pictures of their child and the paparazzi haven’t released any either.

4 Rashida Jones

Shows like The Office and Parks & Recreation would not be the same without Rashida Jones! Interestingly enough, she is another slab who was able to successfully keep her pregnancy a secret from the public. She did not want the public to know about her pregnancy and so, therefore, she never announced it. The news leaked about the birth of her son in September 2018 even though her son was born the month before in August.

3 Zoë Saldana-Perego

Zoë Saldana-Perego married her husband in 2013 and publicly give birth to twins in 2014. For her third child, she decided that she wanted to take a different route and keep things a little bit more personal. She waited until after her son was born to post an Instagram tribute to him for her fans to finally uncover her new family member.

2 Megan Fox

One of the most talked-about people today would have to be Megan Fox due to her brand new relationship with Machine Gun Kelly! Things for Megan Fox are definitely heating up and people all over the world are paying more attention to her now than ever. She used to be married to Brian Austin Green and when rumors of her pregnancy started to swirl, she didn’t speak up to confirm or deny the rumors. She let everyone continue to wonder and curiosity and hid her baby bump with purses and loose clothes.

1 Beyoncé

Beyoncé kept her pregnancy a secret until she was ready to let the world know on her own terms. She waited until she was five months along to make her announcement at the MTV Music Video Awards in 2011. She reveals her baby bump and even gave it a sweet rub while her fans screamed in excitement. The fact that she was able to keep her pregnancy hidden for five months says a lot-- but she has been known to always maintain her privacy anyway even before getting pregnant.
