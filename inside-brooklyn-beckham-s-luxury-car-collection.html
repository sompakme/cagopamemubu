Inside Brooklyn Beckhams Luxury Car Collection

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Quick Links

  • Jeep Wrangler Safari
  • Mercedes C-Class Coupe
  • BMW Z4
  • BMW M3
  • Mercedes-Benz GL 63
  • Jaguar XK140

English model and photographer Brooklyn Beckham is the eldest son of the former footballer David Beckham and Spice Girls' member-turned-fashion designer Victoria Beckham. He may only be just 23 years old but he already got some enviable collection of cars which is mostly from his loving parents. Brooklyn’s generous parents always gifts him car for achieving a milestone in his life, he was given a Mercedes-Benz for passing his test. He was recently married in a lavish and multimillion dollar worth wedding to American actress and billionaire heiress Nicola Peltz just recently.

Newlyweds Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz have left their wedding reception using an open-top two-seater battery-converted 1958 Jaguar XK140 which is a wedding present from Brooklyn’s loving parents David and Victoria. Brooklyn was only seventeen years old when he was quoted whopping $40,000 insurance for his cars Mercedes C-Class coupe and BMW Z4. Take a peek at his luxury car collection.

Jeep Wrangler Safari

Brooklyn was seen driving a Jeep Wrangler Safari while he was still dating actress Chloe Grace Moretz. They were often seen taking a ride at his Jeep Wrangler Safari just near the family home of the Beckhams in California. It is pretty imminent that his tastes in cars were inspired by the cars his father own. David was seen in the past driving around in a Jeep Wrangler with then 11 years old Brooklyn. The Jeep Wrangler Safari offers more opportunities for adventure for its owner with the most customization available to the buyer. Wrangler aims to give the driver of their cars the thrill they want the most while experiencing an open air. The Jeep Wrangler Safari currently retails at $46,000.

Mercedes C-Class Coupe

Brooklyn was seen numerous times strolling around London using his Mercedes C-Class. However being a teenager that he is, at age seventeen, he has been getting numerous parking tickets from his luxury car after he parks his luxury car in different neighborhoods of London. Mercedes C-Class Coupe can go from 0 to 100km/h in the span of 6.7 seconds. He was quoted by Only Young Drivers $40,000 a year to cover his insurance which is among the only two insurance agencies who were willing to cover the car. The cost of one Mercedes C-Class starts at $49,000.

BMW Z4

Brooklyn has always had a working relationship with BMW and even got an opportunity to meet the manufacturer of the car. Brooklyn claims that BMW Z4 is probably the meanest car he has ever seen and it is very fast. He was able to meet the people who designed the car when he went to Munich and they took him through the whole process of production from start until the last operation to produce the car. The BMW Z4 is considered to be a great car with a so-so ranking but offers high quality of service to its owners. It can scoot through turns with much agility while the ride is composed. The BMW Z4 currently retails for $50,000.

BMW M3

Brooklyn was only eighteen years old when he first got his hands on BMW M3 when he was seen driving around Los Angeles with it. The BMW M3 can go from 0 to 100km/h in the span of four seconds and has a maximum speed of 250km/h. Brooklyn has stated on his interviews numerous times that he likes his cars classic and old school. The cost of one BMW M3 starts at $78,000. He loves his BMW M3 and claims that he has never felt so comfortable driving a car than his BMW M3. The M3 has a twin-turbo with 3.0-liter inline-six.

Mercedes-Benz GL 63

Brooklyn has been seen driving around Beverly Hills with his Mercedes-Benz GL 63 AMG. The luxury car exudes refinement with its striking design paired with maximum safety plus its dynamism which is only typical for a Mercedes-Benz car. Mercedes-Benz claims that their GL 63 AMG was built to those who crave all the good things in life such as high quality performance, luxurious driving and exclusivity. This is one of Brooklyn’s most expensive cars as it currently sells for $134,000.

Jaguar XK140

Jaguar XK140 is the latest addition to Brooklyn’s luxury car collection as it was just gifted to him by his loving parents as a wedding gift. David Beckham has been investing in a company called Lunaz which is based near the Silverstone which is known to be the home for the British Grand Prix since 2021. The Jaguar XK140 he has commissioned features some modified brakes with suspension and steering and some additional changes to the dashboard, switchgear and controls. David Lorenz of Lunaz describes the car as a beautiful creation and he stated that he is honored to be the bridge in the gap between the professional life of David Beckham and his family life. This is by far the most expensive car on Brooklyn’s collection as it costs around $500,000.
