Kourtney Kardashian Wears Kim-Esque Fitted Berry Dress, Nude Heels

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 6, 2024

Sister, sister style! Gearing up to film more scenes for her hit series, Kourtney Kardashian hit the TV studio in Los Angeles’ Brentwood nabe on Monday, Sept. 28, and she chose a very Kim Kardashian-esque ensemble for the occasion.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 36, wore a fitted, sleeveless burgundy dress, which she teamed with nude, braided lace-up sandals, compliments of Gianvito Rossi ($995). (Kim, 34, has rocked the same pair for more than one outing.) Kourtney added a bracelet and a black top-handle purse, which she carried in one hand.

And that’s not to mention her beauty choices. The mom of three, who separated from longtime boyfriend Scott Disick this summer, stunned with blushed cheeks, an oxblood statement lip, and a slicked-back ‘do (a style Kim loves to sport).

As for the look-alike Kim ensemble featured here? It’s from August 2014, when the soon-to-be second-time mom donned Michael Costello’s berry-hued dress, paired with nude ankle-strap sandals.

Of course, the style shares go both ways! While Kourtney may have channeled Kim this time, Mrs. Kanye West has openly admitted to stealing her older sister’s pregnancy styles—though, she later confessed that it wasn’t her best sartorial move to date.

“Everyone used to say Kourtney had the best pregnancy style, so I would borrow a dress she wore when she was pregnant, and OMG it did not look the same!” the mom to North West, 2, shared via KimKardashianWest.com. “I also look back at my last pregnancy looks and sometimes I am mortified.”

But she also added, “The side-by-side comparisons were just not good for my soul, so I finally had to figure out my own style that worked for ME!”
