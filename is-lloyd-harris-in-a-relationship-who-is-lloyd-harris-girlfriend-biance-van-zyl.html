Biance Van Zyl is the girlfriend of South African tennis star Lloyd Harris.

Lloyd Harris is used to traveling with his stunning girlfriend Biance Van Zyl.

Biance Van Zyl’s boyfriend Lloyd George Muirhead Harris is a South African professional tennis player.

Lloyd Harris has been ranked as high as World No. 31 in singles by the Association of Tennis Professionals, achieved on 13 September 2021, making him the current African and South African No. 1 men’s singles player.
