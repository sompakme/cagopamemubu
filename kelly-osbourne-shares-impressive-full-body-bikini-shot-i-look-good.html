Kelly, is that you? Kelly Osbourne has no problem showing off her slender frame these days — she worked hard for it, after all! The Fashion Police co-host, 29, who has been in the public eye since she was a surly teen on her family’s reality series The Osbournes, posted a sexy Instagram bikini shot on Saturday, Oct. 11. 

Since her time on the show and dabbling in music (“Shut Up,” anyone?), the lavender-haired star has significantly slimmed down, and she was proud to flaunt her most impressive full-body shot yet.
