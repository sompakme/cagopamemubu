MMA: Casey ONeill Parents

Casey ONeill was born to her parents on October 7, 1997, in Irvine, Scotland.

O’Neill lived in Kilmarnock for the first few years of her childhood.

Her father, Cam, was a professional kickboxer, and she began kickboxing training when she was four years old.

Casey went to Australia’s Gold Coast with her family when she was eleven years old, where she attended Coombabah State High School.

O’Neill began training in other martial arts, such as Brazilian jiu-jitsu when 13 years old.

At the age of 15, her father finally permitted O’Neill to compete in her first amateur MMA bout.

Casey ONeill is Australian by nationality, though born in Scotland, and has a mixed ethnicity of Scottish-Australian.

So far, the 24-year-old champion has an MMA record of eight victories and zero losses.
