We have uncovered the facts about Keanu's new girlfriend, and she is one interesting character. It is easy to see why he chose her based on her interests and their history together. Keep reading to learn 10 facts about Keanu Reeves' new girlfriend Alexandra Grant!

10. She Works As An Artist

Grant's main profession is as an artist, and she has created media in a multitude of forms. She loves painting, sculpting, drawing, and even creating documentaries. Her first solo exhibit was back in 2007 at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Los Angeles, and since then her career has taken off.

She has continued to create a multitude of works and collaborate with other artists on masterpieces. Grant has also taught at the Art Center College of Design from 2009 to 2011, as well as small stints at other colleges in recent years.

9. Grant Is 46-Years-Old

Grant might look a bit older due to her grey hair, but she is only 46-years-old. Keanu, on the other hand, looks young for his age as he is now 55-years-old. Love knows no boundaries as age is just a number, and these two prove it as they have finally revealed themselves to the public.

We hope they both have a happy ending and find the love of their life, whether it is each other or not. It can't be denied though that these two are utterly adorable together, so we hope that they do manage to last.

8. She Is An Ordained Minister

Grant loves to please her friends and be a part of their lives, which is why she became an ordained minister. She was the one who married her friends named Ginna and Edwin in a beautiful ceremony that we know they will never forget.

It is obvious she loves to go the extra mile for the ones she loves, which is why her relationship with Keanu was destined to be. The two form a pair of kind and caring individuals who understand what it means to support those around you.

7. Alexandra Co-Owns A Publishing Company With Keanu

They created a publishing company called X Artists' Books and they focus on those written by artistic minds. It is small, and the two have even collaborated on two separate books together.

They wrote Ode To Happiness and Shadows, and they are both fun and filled with pictures that they hope will inspire others not to take life too seriously. We imagine they will create more works together in the future, especially if their relationship continues to stay serious for the long haul.

6. She Has Lived In Several Countries

Grant has spent time in several countries throughout her childhood as her parents were based in Africa and the Middle East for their jobs as professors and a foreign diplomat. After they divorced, Grant moved with her mother to Mexico City where she entered a multicultural British school.

She then attended school in Missouri for a year before moving to Paris with her mother. She has learned to speak several languages along the way, as she now understands English, Spanish, and French.

5. Fairview Park, Ohio Is Her Hometown

This artist was born in Fairview Park, Ohio, and based on these roots you can already see her ambition. This city is a suburb of Cleveland, Ohio and filled with small shops and cheap houses for rent.

We know she moved around as a child, but she wasn't born into the industry and had to fight her past to make it in the big city. Luckily, she clawed her way to the top and is now the woman who has stolen Keanu's heart.

4. She Founded The grantLOVE Project

This is a non-profit organization that sells artwork and merchandise to support this community. Grant founded it back in 2008 and all of the profits are put into artist projects and other non-profit organizations.

She wants to help others achieve their dreams and share their art with the world. A few of the organizations that this helps include Project Angel Food, Art of Elysium, 18th Street Arts Center, and LAXART. The artist community appreciates her support, and this is one project that we know Reeves can love as well.

3. Grant Has Collaborated With Michael Joyce, Channing Hansen, And Hélène Cixous

She has worked with several artists in multiple fields throughout her career. A few of the more prominent figures include Michael Joyce, Channing Hansen, and Hélène Cixous. Joyce is an author and critic of electronic literature, and he has also held a position as a professor.

Channing Hansen is an artist who focuses on the power of needlework and Hélène Cixous is a French writer. They all worked with Grant on her project called Radical Collaboration and we know we will see her with other creative minds in the future.

2. Her Work Is Featured In Many Galleries

Grant's work has been featured in several exceptional galleries across the world. Her work has been showcased in France, Guatemala, Colorado, Michigan, and California. It is impressive that she has become an international icon in the art industry through her works.

The different galleries that wanted to show her off included Mains d'Œuvres, 20th Bienal de Arte Paiz, Lowell Ryan Projects, and the Los Angeles County Museum of Art. Keanu might be well known in the entertainment industry, but Grant is the superstar in the realm of creativity.

1. She Graduated From Two Colleges With Art Degrees

Grant attended two prestigious colleges in an effort to obtain an art degree. She first attended Swarthmore College, a very selective school, where she earned a Bachelor's in History and Studio Art.

She went back to school 6 years later she went back for her Master of Fine Arts which she received from the California College of the Arts. This is one education that paid off as she is now known across the world for her work, and would not have met Keanu without these talents.
