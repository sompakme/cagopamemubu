Toliver drew inspiration from a diverse range of artists, including the legendary Marvin Gaye, the soulful Sade, the smooth Maxwell, and the iconic Stevie Wonder. These influences helped shape his unique sound and style, blending elements of hip hop, trap, and R&B into his music.
