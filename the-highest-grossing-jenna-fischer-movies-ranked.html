The Highest-Grossing Jenna Fischer Movies, Ranked

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 26, 2024

Quick Links

  • The Promotion - 2008 ($406 Thousand)
  • The Brothers Solomon - 2007 ($900 Thousand)
  • Are You Here - 2014 ($978 Thousand)
  • Brad's Status - 2017 ($3.2 Million)
  • Solitary Man - 2010 ($4.3 Million)
  • Slither - 2006 ($12.9 Million)
  • Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story - 2007 ($20.6 Million)
  • The 15:17 to Paris - 2018 ($56 Million)
  • Hall Pass - 2011 ($87.1 Million)
  • Blades of Glory - 2007 ($145.5 Million)

Step into the spotlight and brace yourself for a star-studded journey through the remarkable career of Jenna Fischer. As we delve into the realm of box office triumphs, prepare to be mesmerized by Fischer's charm, talent, and comedic prowess. From rib-tickling comedies to heartfelt dramas, Fischer has repeatedly proven that she's a force to be reckoned with in the world of cinema. So, grab your popcorn, find a cozy spot, and let's embark on a cinematic adventure like no other. Get ready to explore Jenna Fischer's top 10 highest-grossing movies, where laughter, tears, and unforgettable moments await. It's time to dim the lights and let the magic unfold!

The Promotion - 2008 ($406 Thousand)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Brace yourself for a hilariously low-budget rollercoaster as Jenna Fischer takes on the role of Michele in The Promotion. This laugh-out-loud comedy managed to rack up an impressive $406 thousand at the worldwide box office, proving that Fischer's talent shines regardless of the budget. With her comedic timing and undeniable charm, Fischer effortlessly steals the spotlight and delivers a performance that will leave you grinning from ear to ear. The Promotion offers an absurdly entertaining ride as Fischer proves that big laughs can come in small packages.

The Brothers Solomon - 2007 ($900 Thousand)

Via Flickr/Trailer Addict

Meet the quirky and endearing Michele in The Brothers Solomon. Jenna Fischer brings her signature wit and comedic brilliance to this offbeat comedy, which earned $900 thousand worldwide. With Fischer's impeccable timing and ability to find humor in even the most awkward situations, she effortlessly steals scenes and leaves audiences in stitches. This movie makes for a delightful and delightfully odd adventure that proves Fischer's comedic genius knows no bounds.

Are You Here - 2014 ($978 Thousand)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Jenna Fischer shines as Alli in the thought-provoking comedy Are You Here. This witty exploration of friendship, family, and self-discovery may not have set the box office on fire, but Fischer's performance is an undeniable highlight. With her natural charm and ability to balance humor and emotion, Fischer elevates the film and leaves a lasting impression. Fans are captivated by Fischer's nuanced portrayal and they find themselves pondering life's complexities long after the credits roll.

Brad's Status - 2017 ($3.2 Million)

Via Flickr/Catchplay

In the introspective comedy-drama Brad's Status, Jenna Fischer delivers a heartfelt performance as part of an ensemble cast. While the film may not have shattered box office records, Fischer's portrayal as a supportive wife adds depth and warmth to the story. As Fischer's character navigates the challenges of life alongside her husband, she brings a genuine authenticity that resonates with audiences, making for an introspective journey that reminds us of the power of love and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Solitary Man - 2010 ($4.3 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Brace yourself for a captivating performance from Jenna Fischer in the drama-comedy Solitary Man. While the film may not have achieved blockbuster status, Fischer's portrayal of a caring daughter adds a layer of emotional depth. As she shares the screen with a stellar ensemble cast, Fischer's presence shines brightly and leaves a lasting impact. With her ability to convey complex emotions and genuine vulnerability, Fischer proves that sometimes the most outstanding performances come from the heart.

Slither - 2006 ($12.9 Million)

Via Flickr/Plex

Get ready for a wild ride as Jenna Fischer dives into the horror-comedy genre in Slither. In this creature feature, Fischer takes on the role of Shelby, a small-town girl caught amid an alien invasion. While the film may not have achieved astronomical box office success, Fischer's performance is a standout. With her ability to balance screams and laughter, she adds a refreshing dynamic to the genre.

Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story - 2007 ($20.6 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Jenna Fischer joins the musical comedy Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story with a bang. As Darlene Madison Cox, she shines alongside an ensemble cast in this hilarious parody of biographical films. While the film may not have broken box office records, Fischer's comedic talents are on full display. With her pitch-perfect timing and infectious energy, she delivers laughs and memorable moments. Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story provides a toe-tapping, side-splitting journey through the life of Dewey Cox.

The 15:17 to Paris - 2018 ($56 Million)

Via Flickr/The Week

Jenna Fischer takes on the role of Heidi Skarlatos in the gripping true story The 15:17 to Paris. While the film may have faced mixed reviews, Fischer's portrayal adds a touch of heart and authenticity. As she shares the screen with real-life heroes, Fischer's performance is a reminder of the courage and resilience of ordinary people. This movie is a thrilling and emotionally charged ride that highlights Fischer's ability to bring depth and humanity to her characters.

Hall Pass - 2011 ($87.1 Million)

Via Flickr/Hungama

Prepare for hilarity and hijinks as Jenna Fischer joins the comedy Hall Pass. In this outrageous tale of marital freedom, Fischer's portrayal of Maggie adds a touch of heart and relatability. While the film may not have received critical acclaim, Fischer's comedic timing and endearing presence make her a standout. With her ability to balance laughter and warmth, she proves that love and laughter go hand in hand.

Blades of Glory - 2007 ($145.5 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Jenna Fischer takes to the ice in the uproarious comedy Blades of Glory. As Katie Van Waldenberg, she joins forces with an ensemble cast in this figure skating extravaganza. While the film may not have landed a triple axel at the box office, Fischer's performance is a delight. With her impeccable comedic timing and undeniable chemistry with her co-stars, she glides effortlessly across the ice and leaves audiences in stitches.
