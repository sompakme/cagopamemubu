Kendall J. and More Cinch Street Styles With Gucci Belts

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

Good style is a cinch with this accessory! Kendall Jenner, Dakota Johnson, Jennifer Aniston, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Whitney Port are all finishing their fall outfits with the Gucci Leather Belt with Double G Buckle ($420, gucci.com). The black leather band featuring gold accents is the perfect way to add a bit of polish to the most casual or dressiest street style looks. Keep reading to see how these ladies wore theirs.

Kendall Jenner

For an afternoon of shopping in Beverly Hills October 12, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star bundled up in a cropped leopard-print jacket layered over a white printed T-shirt and black flared jeans. Round sunglasses, patent leather booties and a plastic choker added vintage vibes.

Dakota Johnson

The Fifty Shades actress gave her plain white T-shirt and blue skinny jeans a luxurious touch with a bucket tote, booties and round sunglasses while out in Los Angeles October 5.

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

Black and tan! The supermodel topped her black shirt, straight-leg jeans and booties with a light brown coat September 17 in New York City.

Jennifer Aniston

Cute as a button: The Friends alum tucked a white blouse into Mother the Cruiser jeans with wedges September 30 in New York City.
