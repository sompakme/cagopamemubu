WWE Fastlane Betting Odds Favor Alexa Bliss Over Randy Orton, Advertised Match Might Be Off

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 12, 2024

The WWE Fastlane betting odds are quite interesting. Not only do they indicate that Randy Orton will come up short against Alexa Bliss, but they also indicate that Braun Strowman vs. Shane McMahon might not happen despite being advertised on Monday for the WWE Network/Peacock special.

Below are the current betting odds for WWE Fastlane courtesy of BetOnline:

Roman Reigns              -1000   (1/10)

Daniel Bryan                 +525     (21/4)

Shayna Baszler & Nia Jax                      -300     (1/3)

Sasha Banks & Bianca Belair                 +200     (2/1)

Drew McIntyre               -550     (2/11)

Sheamus                      +325     (13/4)

Alexa Bliss                    -200     (1/2)

Randy Orton                 +150     (3/2)

Big E                            -300     (1/3)

Apollo Crews                 +200     (2/1)

Braun Strowman                        -150     (2/3)

Shane McMahon                       +110     (11/10)

Note: This match is rumored to no longer be taking place.
