Lil Wayne Says Goodbye To His Miami Mansion For $28 Million

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Lil Wayne made a big profit after he sold his Miami mansion for $28 million. The rapper was hoping for a slightly higher selling price, as he listed the estate for $29.5 million last September. However, he accepted a slightly lower offer at the beginning of February.

The stunning property features 7-bedrooms and 11-bathrooms. It features over 10,00 square feet of living space. Some of the home’s most stunning features include a variety of tropical landscape, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a chef’s kitchen with separate staff quarters.

The estate features a half-acre of land, and it backs directly onto Biscayne Bay, allowing for stunning ocean views from all angles of the house. It also comes with 100 feet of private water frontage, which provides space to dock a boat as well as kayaks and jet skis.

The mansion is located on Allison Island, an ultra-luxurious private island with 35-actres of land. It’s a well-known spot for famous folks looking to escape the paparazzi.

Lil Wayne moved into the custom estate after it was built in 2017. He originally paid $16.8 million at the time, so he made over $10 million in profit from the recent sale.

But the entertainer reportedly made a lot of additions to the home, which explains the expensive selling price.

He reportedly installed an “ open-air cabana, outdoor kitchen, and a reflecting pool ” during his time living in the mansion.

Lil Wayne isn’t living too far away, but it is something more modest, for a mansion, that is. He reportedly lives full-time in his Hidden Hills estate. He purchased the mansion for $15.4 million in 2021, and it was a brand-new, custom build at the time.

With over 12,000 square feet of living space, Lil Wayne likely isn’t pining for more room. It features 7 bedrooms and 7.5 bathrooms, as well as sprawling panoramic views. There’s also a 1-bedroom guest house in the back.

Some of the mansion’s features include a chef’s kitchen, refrigerated wine display, entertainment lounge, and a home theatre. There are also plenty of outdoor amenities, too, including a basketball court, pool, and a variety of seating areas. Overall, the estate features 3.26 acres of land.

The home is reportedly close to a property that Kylie Jenner owns in the same gated community.
