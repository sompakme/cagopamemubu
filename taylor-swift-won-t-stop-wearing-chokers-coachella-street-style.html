Taylor Swift Won't Stop Wearing Chokers: Coachella, Street Style

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

https://www.instagram.com/p/BERUJqLjvOO/

Taylor Swift‘s chokers will never go out of style!

The “New Romantics” singer, 26, can’t stop wearing the ’90s-inspired necklaces and most recently rocked four different kinds in the span of one day.

“I feel like maybe chokers are the new flower crowns?” she mused in a Saturday, April 16, Instagram, in which she and all her friends, including Victoria’s Secret Angel Martha Hunt and her brother, Austin Swift, were wearing the Kardashian-loved trend.

Coachillllin ?

A post shared by Martha Hunt (@marthahunt) on Apr 16, 2016 at 11:10am PDT

In the pic, Swift, who debuted a new bleach blonde hair color on Friday, April 15, wore the Dannijo Wren choker.

Later that night, the “Wildest Dreams” singer was spotted in a bejeweled, star-themed collar necklace, which jazzed up her plain gray tee by The Great, and even repurposed the baubles as anklets. One Erin Dana Rusted Silver Python Choker was wrapped around each of Swift’s ankles while she walked around the festival’s Neon Carnival, making her ensemble chock-full of chokers.

And before she touched down in California that same day, she was spotted adding extra sparkle to a white shift dress at her backup singer’s wedding in San Antonio, Texas, wearing an Erickson Beamon choker.
