Exes Cardi B, Offset Booked For NYE Performances At Same Miami Hotel

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Newly separated rappers, Cardi B and Offset, are booked for New Year’s Eve performances at the same hotel, Page Six reports.

The estranged couple will perform at different venues inside Fontainebleau Miami Beach, with Offset at the hotel’s club LIV and Cardi at Luxe, its poolside venue. Apparently, the booking was made before they broke up.

According to club Luxe’s site, tickets are ranging from $5,000 up to $25,000 for stage-front cabanas. For Offset’s booking, tickets are going from $125 to $15,000.

Cardi B confirmed breakup rumors while on Instagram Live today (Dec. 11).

After social media learned that Cardi unfollowed Offset, the “Up” rapper revealed that she’s “been single for a minute now.” Additionally, Set had been accused of cheating with reality star Chrisean Rock by Blueface — which Cardi said she doesn’t believe.

“I don’t care to find out because I’ve been single for a minute now,” she revealed to her social media followers last week. She also claimed Offset has been “doing [her] dirty,” after she helped him with his latest album.

“You’ve really been feeling yourself… because of your bi**h a** album and sh*t,” she said in her explosive rant. “And you really been doing me dirty after so many f**king years that I mothe**king helped your a**. Not even a f**king thank you that I got from your bi**h a**.”

“This motherf**ker really like to play games with me when I’m at my most vulnerable time, when I’m not the most confident, he like to play games with me because he knows I’m not an easy girl,” she added. “Yesterday, I could’ve been out…he knows I’m in my house…and I really been sparring you.”
