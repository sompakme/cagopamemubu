Top WWE Tag Team Appears On 2/20 NXT TV Tapings *Spoilers*

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 6, 2024

The Good Brothers made a stop on NXT Tuesday night.

Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows, The Good Brothers, had been largely absent from WWE programming in the past few months. They made an appearance (with AJ Styles) on the December 15 episode of SmackDown, but haven’t competed much on television since their return to the company the year prior.

The pair appeared at Tuesday night’s NXT television tapings, a possible sign of a new direction for The Good Brothers. This set of tapings will air on February 20.

*Spoilers follow*

New NXT Tag Team Champions Bron Breakker and Baron Corbin cut a promo. However, Andre Chase and Duke Hudson interrupted and claimed they were owed a title shot. Nathan Frazer and Axiom came out to the ring and made their case for a title shot. In response, Ava Raine booked a #1 contender’s match between them.

Chase and Hudson picked up the win over Nathan Frazer and Axiom to earn a shot at the NXT Tag Team Championship. However, The Good Brothers attacked both teams after the bell.

The Good Brothers have competed sporadically in 2023 on WWE live event cards. However, Karl Anderson’s last televised match was a loss to Jimmy Uso on September 29, 2023. Gallows’ last televised match was a tag team match loss to the Street Profits on the August 18, 2023 episode of WWE SmackDown.

The February 20 NXT taping marks the team’s first appearance in NXT since October 2022. The Good Brothers also competed on the November 6, 2019 episode of NXT, their first-ever appearance together (and Karl’s first-ever) on Tuesday nights.
