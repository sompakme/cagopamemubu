Justin Bieber, Khlo Kardashian, T-Pain, Jelly Roll, More Pictured With WWE Golden Title

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

A number of celebrities have gotten their hands on Snoop Dogg’s WWE Golden Title ahead of Super Bowl LVIII. 

Snoop Dogg’s WWE Golden Title previously went missing, and it has gone on quite a journey ever since. It has been bouncing around Las Vegas ahead of the Super Bowl. On social media, WWE has shared images in which various celebrities can be seen with the title.

Justin Bieber was seen wearing the gold around his waist.

Khloé Kardashian also posed with the championship.

T-Pain, Tiffany Haddish, and Quavo also took a picture with the title.

Additionally, Jelly Roll, Diplo, Nina Dobrev, and French Montana held the title at one point.

The WWE Golden Title was also spotted at the star-studded NFL Honors ceremony.

Fans will have to wait and see where the WWE Golden Title will wind up next.
