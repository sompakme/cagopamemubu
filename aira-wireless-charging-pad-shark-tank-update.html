The Aira Wireless Charging Pad uses Qi wireless charging technology to transfer power from the charging pad to compatible devices through electromagnetic induction.

Yes, the Aira Wireless Charging Pad can charge multiple devices simultaneously, making it a convenient solution for households or offices with multiple devices.
