Early Life and Wrestling Career

Chad Mendes, born in Hanford, California in 1985, had a passion for wrestling from an early age. He honed his skills in high school and later pursued his wrestling career at Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo.

Mendes’ college wrestling career was nothing short of impressive. With an overall record of 64-14, he demonstrated his talent and dedication to the sport. During his time in college, Mendes earned the prestigious NCAA All-American honors twice, showcasing his exceptional skills on the mat.

His success in college wrestling laid the foundation for his future achievements in the world of mixed martial arts.

Entry into Mixed Martial Arts

After completing his college wrestling career, Chad Mendes embarked on a new chapter in his athletic journey by venturing into the world of mixed martial arts (MMA). Equipped with his formidable skills and the desire to make a name for himself, Mendes joined forces with Team Alpha Male, a renowned MMA team based in Sacramento.

Under the guidance of experienced coaches and training partners, Mendes honed his techniques and pushed his boundaries. The rigorous training regimen allowed him to harness his raw talent and transform into a well-rounded MMA athlete.

In 2010, Mendes made his debut in the World Extreme Cagefighting (WEC) organization, showcasing his wrestling prowess and overall athleticism. As the WEC merged with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), Mendes seamlessly transitioned into the world’s premier MMA organization.

In 2011, Mendes had his highly anticipated UFC debut, facing off against Michihiro Omigawa in a featherweight bout. Displaying his exceptional skills in the octagon, Mendes emerged victorious, securing a unanimous decision win.

“Training with Team Alpha Male has been instrumental in my development as an MMA fighter. The support and camaraderie within the team have pushed me to new heights, and I am grateful for the opportunity to represent such a respected organization.”

Chad Mendes’ success in his MMA debut and subsequent fights cemented his status as a rising star in the featherweight division. His strong foundation in wrestling, coupled with his relentless dedication to training, provided him with a solid platform for success.

Throughout his career, Mendes continued to evolve as an MMA fighter, utilizing his wrestling background to dominate opponents and showcase his versatile skill set.

Training with Team Alpha Male

Training with Team Alpha Male, a renowned MMA gym founded by Urijah Faber, allowed Chad Mendes to tap into a wealth of knowledge and experience. The team consists of elite fighters who share a common goal of pushing the boundaries of their craft.

Under the guidance of coaches like Master Thong, Fabio “Pateta” Prado, and Justin Buchholz, Mendes underwent intense training sessions to improve his striking, grappling, conditioning, and overall fight IQ. The team’s emphasis on high-level wrestling further complemented Mendes’ existing skill set, enabling him to dominate opponents with takedowns and ground control.

The collaborative environment at Team Alpha Male, where fighters push each other to their limits, fostered Mendes’ growth as an athlete. The team’s supportive culture and commitment to excellence played a pivotal role in his success in the MMA world.
