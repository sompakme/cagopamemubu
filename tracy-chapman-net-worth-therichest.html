American singer-songwriter Tracy Chapman has an estimated net worth of $8 million. The multi-platinum and Grammy award-winning artist was born in Cleveland, Ohio on the 30th of March 1964 and was raised by her mother who had a great role to play in developing her music skills. Chapman could play the ukulele by the time she was three years old and the guitar by eight. At this time she had started writing various songs, developing her song writing and singing skills.

Chapman graduated from Tufts University but whilst studying she started singing in various coffeehouses where she came to the attention of different professionals in the music industry and eventually signed a contract with Elektra Records after graduating. Chapman has proved herself in the music industry and her lyrics have expressed her passion for activism.

To date, Chapman has turned her attention to activism, where she speaks on behalf of many organizations, often performing to raise money and awareness for various causes.
