Heres Why Fans Think Erika Jayne Invested In Lisa Rinnas Beauty Line

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Fans of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills are heavily invested in the drama of season 11. There are questions surrounding whether Erika Jayne spends Tom Girardi's money and the cast talks about Erika's divorce in many episodes.

Now that Erika's co-star Lisa Rinna has released her own line of beauty products, fans are wondering if Erika has invested in Rinna Beauty. Let's take a look.

Did It Happen?

Lisa Rinna has celebrated releasing her beauty brand at 57 years old, and Lisa Rinna's Watch What Happens Live appearance gave fans some juicy details. Someone asked the reality star, "Are you not making Erika own it [this season] because she invested in your makeup line?"

Lisa said that despite the rumors that are going around, this isn't something that happened at all.

Lisa said, "[Erika] did not invest in [Rinna Beauty], let's get that really straight right now. That was a very terrible rumor. I mean, I would never borrow money from anybody. I have a partner that I'm in business with, Erika did not give me a dime. And by the way, I would never borrow money from a friend. Don't ever borrow money from a friend — don't ever get into business with a friend!"

Lisa Rinna's advice is definitely smart as going into business with friends can be tricky, and so can asking them for money.

Fans also think that Lisa Rinna should question Erika Jayne more on this season of RHOBH. According to Us Weekly, Lisa said that she is certain that Erika was in the dark about what was going on. Lisa explained, “I’ve known Erika a long time, I don’t think she knew anything. … Why would Tom Girardi tell Erika anything? The lawyers didn’t even know. That is how I feel, I’m going to stand by it. If I’m wrong, I’ll f—king deal with it.”

Lisa also said, “Do I have to carry the show on my back? No. … Seriously though, there are other people here, there are eight of us.”

Rinna Beauty

Lisa Rinna came out with Rinna Beauty in November 2020, and Lisa's brand has lip kits that are definitely a perfect fit for the actress and reality star, since people always talk about Lisa's lips.

The lip kits have really fun names, from No Apologies to Birthday Suit to Legends Only, and the colors range from nude to pink to red.

Lisa Rinna shared that her mom always wore lipstick and it sounds like that has been a huge inspiration. Fans of RHOBH have seen Lisa's mom Lois appear in some episodes, and she's always sweet and totally fabulous.

In an interview with Page Six, Lisa said, “My mom was always someone who had her face on, and that meant lipstick. She never left the house without it. All I remember, from the earliest age, is that was what made my mom feel comfortable going out into the world.”

It sounds like this has been something that Lisa Rinna has really wanted to do: she told Forbes.com, “This was always a dream of mine and a passion. I had my lips done when I was 24 years old. I always call myself ‘the lip pioneer’ which is so silly and fun and stupid and funny. Why can’t I make money off of this? Why can’t I build a brand?”

The lip kits have been getting lots of buzz, with Lisa telling Allure.com that she wanted to come out with a lip product around a decade ago. She said that things kept going wrong and since she has been selling clothes on QVC for a while, she figured that maybe that was where she had to put her attention. Thankfully, it totally worked out and it seems like Lisa is really enjoying it.

Lisa's 'RHOBH' Time

Lisa Rinna's seasons of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills have been fascinating to watch. She had conflict with Kim Richards for a long time, and in season 11, she has talked about her daughter Amelia dating the older Scott Disick.

Fans have also gotten the chance to see Lisa and Harry Hamlin hanging out at home in some scenes, and Lisa introduced viewers to Harry's beautiful garden in their backyard.
