Heres How Demi Lovato Spends Her $40 Million Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

Highlights

  • Demi Lovato regrets starting her career at a young age, but the time in the spotlight paid off with a $40 million net worth.
  • Lovato recently sold her $8.9 million home and purchased a farmhouse in Studio City.
  • Lovato has invested in a rehab clinic, owns an impressive collection of cars, and has donated $125K to help combat the pandemic.

Demi Lovato has been in the spotlight her entire career since she appeared on shows like Barney. Despite her massive net worth, Lovato does have regrets when it came to starting so young.

"I wouldn’t start that young if I could do it over again," Lovato explained. "I don’t regret anything, but it was difficult to transition from being a child star to transforming into a mainstream artist. It’s something that is very challenging."

Nonetheless, the time in the spotlight paid off in a different way, as Lovato was able to rack in a $40 million net worth, a number that will only grow moving forward.

We’ll take a look at what else Lovato is spending her money on these days.

Updated September 2023: Demi Lovato has been a part of the entertainment industry for decades. As such, she has been able to amass her $40 million fortune over that period.

Much of that net worth has come from touring. Given that Lovato has stated that she would no longer be touring, she will have to find other sources to make income if Lovato believes she needs to continue to grow her monetary assets.

With a $40 million net worth, Lovato does not really have to worry about curbing her day-to-day purchases but larger items like homes, cars, and luxury goods may need to have a second thought to ensure that the money Lovato has made for herself lasts and keeps her comfortable for a lifetime.

11 Demi Lovato's Hollywood Hills $8.9 Million Home Sold For $8.25 Million

Lovato has expensive taste when it comes to her home of choice. Turns out, however, she recently sold the location. The mansion had quite the price tag with a value of $8.9 million attached to the property.

“Perched at the end of a cul-de-sac, the estate spans over an acre," the listing states. "The main draw is a modern home of 5,500 square feet, and other highlights include a swimming pool, spa, turf lawn and dining gazebo."

Justin Bieber was apparently interested in the property but it appears he looked elsewhere for his final purchase.

Since the sale of her home, Lovato purchased a farmhouse in Studio City, Ca, which was a blank slate for Lovato to turn into anything she could imagine.

10 Demi Lovato Bought Shares In A Rehab Clinic

Lovato made an emotional investment, becoming a part-owner of a rehab clinic, CAST out in Los Angeles. It was the same clinic Lovato attended when she decided to get treatment and by all accounts, the investment was done was the best intentions in mind.

8 Demi Lovato Donated $125K To Help Combat The Pandemic

Similar to so many other celebs, Lovato stepped up in a big way, donating $125K towards the pandemic fund. The reason why she did it was simple. It was to do her part to give strength and inspiration to those who needed it during a time when the world was in chaos.

“Recently, we’ve watched our world change and have thought hard about holding off on the launch," Lovato stated. "After sitting down to reflect for a few days, we realized there’s no better time to inspire others with my capsule, which has always been about channeling your inner strength. In addition to lifting up those in need right now, it’s so important for us all to feel empowered, invincible, and strong from the inside out."

7 Demi Lovato Partnered With Fabletics

Lovato only invests in companies that she believes in. While she has her own clothing brand, she also partnered up with Fabletics, where she designed some unique fitness clothing that reflected her personality. The designs were also made so that women could feel comfortable while they worked out.

"This collection is inspired by my personal style - on stage, at home and in the gym, so I had a lot of fun playing with bold colors, prints, and performance elements that I love," Lovato explained. "I felt more confident through the design process this time around, and think that the collection reflects that."

6 Demi Lovato Enjoys Shopping Sprees At Barneys

When Lovato received her first Grammy nomination, she took to Barneys in New York for a major shopping spree. And with a net worth like Lovato has, her shopping adventure likely did not even put a dent in her fortune.

5 Demi Lovato's Luxury Bag Purchases

The Purse Blog listed some of Lovato’s pricier bags and let’s just say they don’t come cheap – some alone are worth more than certain cars traveling on the road. Her premium bag includes the Gucci Medium Babouska Heart Tone, Burberry Studded Elly Hobo, and Prada Vitello Shine Bowler Handbag.

Lovato has only upped her bag collection since, with more top-tier brand names hanging off her shoulder.

4 Demi Lovato Shops At Whole Foods

Lovato certainly has her favorite spots when it comes to grocery shopping, one of them has to be Whole Foods. It is not surprising that this grocery store is where she is typically spotted at, especially given her healthy lifestyle.

Lovato loves the little things in life like buying her favorite type of tea.

"So excited!!!" Lovato stated. "My Texas Tea is in Whole Foods Market!! Have you had it before??"

3 Demi Lovato Signed With Scooter Braun

Lovato made a bold decision in 2019, signing with a management group which is headed by Scooter Braun. She joined the likes of Justin Bieber on the team. At the time, Lovato discussed her joy in making the big investment to Scooter and his team via her IG account and couldn't wait to get started working.

“Dreams came true today for me," Lovato explained. "I officially have a NEW MANAGER!!! And not just any new manager but the one and only @scooterbraun!!!! Couldn’t be happier, inspired and excited to begin this next chapter with you Scooter!!! Thank you for believing in me and for being a part of this new journey. Let’s DO THIS!"

Since then, Lovato and other artists have jumped ship and are no longer working with Braun. Some artists who left the management company of late include Idina Menzel, Ariana Grande, and reportedly, Bieber is leaving his long-time manager as well.

2 Demi Lovato Sends Herself Flowers

It came with a heavy heart, but in March, 2019 Lovato decided to separate from Henry Levy. Lovato became overwhelmed trying to balance a relationship and hectic schedule all at once.

Needing to heal like everyone else, Lovato decided to treat herself. She did so by picking up a bouquet of flowers. Lovato stated she bought the flowers "cause sometimes you gotta send yourself flowers."

1 Demi Lovato Buys The Little Things Like The Rest Of Us

Lovato may be a celebrity but she also needs the daily essentials like everyone else.

We tend to forget, but celebs do the little things just like us. As such, they may be grocery shopping, pop into a local drug store, getting gas, or any of the other chores and errands that their fans have to do for themselves as well.
