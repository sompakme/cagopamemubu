Tom Cruise Put His Famous Multi-Million Dollar Ranch Up For Sale

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Tom Cruise just put his massive ranch up for sale for far more money than any other property located in the area. It's going to take a very special kind of buyer to take this gorgeous estate off his hands, as Tom Cruise is asking $39.5 million, while the properties surrounding him are valued within the $1.9 million price range. This ranch is enormous, spanning over 320 acres in total. The main residence is a 10,000 square foot mansion, and there's also a 1,512 square foot guest house on the property. In order to reach this stunning home, guests must journey up a 1-mile long driveway. Everything about this stunning estate is unique, and believe it or not, the buyer is getting a great discount on the property.  According to Sotheby's Realty, Tom Cruise tried listing this estate for $59 million in 2014 and seems to be willing to let it go for just $39.5 million on a quick sale.

The property holds nostalgic significance, as it is the place in which Tom Cruise conducted his last interview with Oprah. This is also the home he shared with his now-ex-wife Katie Holmes, and their daughter Suri.

According to Vanity Fair, this ranch-style home "sits atop an aspen grove-covered escarpment with sweeping views of the mountainous landscape. The cabin Cruise helped design is covered in bleached cedar timber and native stone with beamed ceilings and large glass walls to give uninterrupted views of the surrounding nature."

The estate is tucked away in a very private region, with most people completely unaware of its existence. Privacy was definitely top of mind when purchasing and designing the home, which even boasts a private entrance to the mountains. The private residential gates lead to the mountains of Uncompahgre National Forest, enabling the new homeowner to adventure through this natural paradise without having to encounter the general public.

The main residence is enormous and features an oversized kitchen and dining area, as well as a library. The ceilings are high, the rooms are bright and spacious, and the views from the massive windows are breath-taking.

Just as impressive as the interior of the main residence is the serene privacy of the natural elements surrounding this ranch. There's a multi-functional sports court that leads to the private trail to the mountains, and the landscaping is immaculately maintained.

This stunning estate is a picture-perfect hideaway that offers every possible amenity, requiring limited trips outside of the home. It's perfect for a home buyer in search of a private oasis with plenty of room to stretch out.

Finding someone that can afford to live as luxuriously as Tom Cruise did when he lived in this home, may pose a bit of a challenge.
