Get Over It

Get Over It is a film released on March 9, 2001 and was distributed by Miramax, and Ignite Entertainment. The film has grossed $11.5 million domestically and has made about $19.8 million on the worldwide box office. The movie directed by Tommy O'Haver was loosely based on A Midsummer Night's Dream where the story revolves around the high school student who is down on his luck, Berke Landers. He lost his girlfriend and he swore to do everything he can just to get her back.

Good Burger

Good Burger is a film released on July 25, 1997 and was distributed by Paramount Pictures Studios, and Nickelodeon Movies. The film which was only released domestically has made about $23.7 million on the box office. The movie directed by Brian Robbins is about the burger-obsessed Ed and sneaky Dexter who realized that they need to fight for Mondo Burger to keep the burger fast food joint going.

Disaster Movie

Disaster Movie is a film released on August 29, 2008 and was distributed by Lionsgate Films, and FilmFlex. The film made $14.1 million on the domestic gross and has made $36.7 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The movie directed by Jason Friedberg I about a group of attractive friends who are in their 20s which are played by Vanessa Minnillo, Kim Kardashian and Matt Lanter. On one fateful night, they must dodge a series of both manmade and natural disasters.

Meet the Spartans

Meet the Spartans is a film released on January 25, 2008 and was distributed by 20th Century Studios. The film has made about $38.2 million domestically and has grossed $84.6 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The film is about Xerxes played by Ken Davitian who is the evil god king of Persia. He sent his massive army to King Leonidas, Sparta and 13 strapping warriors who defended their homeland.

Date Movie

Date Movie is a film released on February 17, 2006 and was distributed by 20th Century Studios, and FilmFlex. The film has made about $48.5 million domestically and has grossed $85.1 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The film directed by Jason Friedberg and Aaron Seltzer is about a hopeless romantic Julia played by American actress Alyson Hannigan who finally found the man of her dreams, the British gentleman named Grant Fonkyerdoder. Just like any other couple, they are faced with a lot hurdles in their relationship.

Epic Movie

Epic Movie is a film released on January 26, 2007 and was released by 20th Century Studios, and FilmFlex. The film has made $39.7 million domestically and has made about $86.8 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The movie directed by Jason Friedberg is about the four adult orphans played by Adam Campbell, Faune Chambers, Kal Penn and Jayma Mays. They have an incredible adventure as a group and decided to visit some bizarre confectioner's chocolate factory.

Cheaper by the Dozen 2

Cheaper by the Dozen 2 is a film released on December 21, 2005 and was distributed by 20th Century Studios, and FilmFlex. The film has made $82.5 million domestically and has made about $135 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The movie directed by Adam Shankman is about Tom Baker played by American actor Steve Martin and his wife Kate played by Bonnie Hunt, who took their kids for a relaxing summer vacation at the lakeside resort. Little did the family know that they will be experiencing their worst vacation of their life.

Starsky & Hutch

Starsky & Hutch is a film released on March 5, 2004 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures, Miramax, Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, and Dimension Films. The film has made $88.2 million domestically and $170 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The movie directed by Todd Philips is about the high-strung workaholic David Starsky played by Ben Stiller and the laidback ladies’ man Ken played by Owen Wilson. The two are detectives who usually patrol some crime-ridden streets of the Bay City, California.

Scary Movie 4

Scary Movie 4 is a film released on April 14, 2006 and was distributed by Dimension Films, Miramax, The Weinstein Company, and Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The film has made $90.7 million domestically and about $178 .7 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The film is about the dimwitted Cindy Campbell played by American actress Anna Faris and her sex-crazed friend named Brenda played by Regina Hall who teamed up with some cute but clueless Tom Ryan played by Craig Bierko.

Scary Movie

Scary Movie is a film released on July 7, 2000 and was distributed by Dimension Films, Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, and Miramax Home Entertainment. The film has made $157 million domestically and has made $277.2 million on the overall ticket sales worldwide. The film directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans is a scary film which parodies numerous films boldly including I Know What You Did Last Summer, The Matrix, The Blair Witch Project, Scream and The Sixth Sense.
