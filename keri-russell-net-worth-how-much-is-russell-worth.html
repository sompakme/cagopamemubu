Early Life and Career Beginnings

Keri Russell was born on March 23, 1976, in Fountain Valley, California. From a young age, she displayed a passion for the performing arts, particularly dance. This early opportunity helped kickstart her entertainment career and set the stage for her future success.

Being part of the “All-New Mickey Mouse Club” exposed Russell to the world of entertainment and provided her with valuable experience and exposure. This platform allowed her to hone her skills and paved the way for future opportunities in the industry.
