Airrack Girlfriend, Is He In A Relationship With Hope Lavine?

Airrack aka Eric Decker is apparently dating Hope Lavine for over four years now.

On September 29, 2020, the couple celebrated their two-year anniversary by posting an appreciation post for the time they had spent together.

She referred to those years as an exciting adventure filled with airport runs, FaceTime dates, naps, surprises, trips to Fellini’s, smart car rides, long walks, and sweet talks.

Lavine’s Instagram profile contains a plethora of photos of the couple, all of which show them spending quality time together. Decker’s on the other hand, has a bare minimum of it.

Many people assumed that the couple had separated after seeing this, but this is not the case; their relationship is still going strong.

They spent 2022 Valentine’s Day together, and on August 8, Lavine posted a photo of him with the caption “My Man.”

LaVine is an independent bridal stylist and blogger with 6 years of experience in the industry. She holds a degree in Marketing and Sales Management.

She worked as a bridal stylist at her local bridal shop for four years before starting her own business. Lori from TLC’s Say Yes to the Dress: Atlanta then offered her a job at Bridals.

LaVine worked at Bridals by Lori for a year before leaving to pursue a career in marketing and sales at IBM. The pandemic struck about 9 months into her job at IBM, and her team was laid off a few months later.

She found herself at a fork in the road. After a lot of brainstorming with her partner, Eric, she landed on the intersection of a bridal fashion blog and marketing.

Lavine now successfully runs her bridal fashion business under the website, hopelavine.com. She has established herself as a bridal fashion icon.

She also writes an interesting blog which can be found on her website.

Airrack Is A Comedic Vlogging YouTuber

Eric Decker, also known as “Airrack,” is a Youtube with 9.93 million subscribers at the moment. Forbes stated it is faster than most creators in the platform’s 17-year history.

His videos range from cycling 38 miles on ice to assisting subscribers in opening a five-star restaurant. Some believe he is the next Mr. Beast. Others compare him to a modern Nathan For You.

However, Forbes discovered that his journey as a creator is eerily similar to Elon Musk’s journey as an entrepreneur.

He is a YouTuber known for his comedic vlogging and his “couch series” collaboration with Logan Paul. His interaction with Paul on the other hand has a different story.

Airrack flew to Miami in January 2020 where Paul was present for his brother Jake’s boxing match against AnEsonGiband. He pretended to be a reporter in order to convince Logan to work with him.

He spent thousands of dollars to fly to Miami and book a hotel room, with no guarantee of meeting Logan.

He was then known for making expensive vlogs and challenge videos in the style of MrBeast, but with different video ideas and on a smaller scale.

Airrack’s videographer and editor from North Carolina is believed to be responsible for much of the channel’s success in 2020. He’s responsible for the iconic Airrack graphics and fast-paced storytelling.

Airrack learned to optimize his approach to meeting and collaborating with other creators as their channel grew.

Prior to YouTube, Airrack ran a successful wedding videography business, which provided him with the necessary experience and funds to launch his channel.

Eric has recently launched a pizza sauce called “Pizzafy,” launched a merch line, and hosted a YouTuber ping-pong tournament.

He is next co-founding Creator Now with his managers Zack Honarvar and Kate Ward. There, they are assisting in the development of the next generation of creators.

Where Is Airrack From?

Airrack is a comedian Youtuber originally from Georgia.

He aspired to be a full-fledged YouTuber, and he simply decided that 2020 would be the year. He achieved his goal of 1 million subscribers by 2021.

He has nearly 10 million subscribers in just two years of time. Eric stated that his initial goal was to reach 20 million subscribers in five years, but after this year’s success, he believes he can do better.

His new mission is to “become one of the biggest YouTubers on the planet.” Furthermore, he intends to focus on other platforms in order to build his brand and eventually break into the top 20 YouTube creators in the world.

Eric will continue to release as much groundbreaking content as possible in the coming years, which he believes is part of the key to his success.
