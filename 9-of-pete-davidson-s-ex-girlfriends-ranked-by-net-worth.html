9 Of Pete Davidsons Ex-Girlfriends, Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 12, 2024

Highlights

  • Pete Davidson has been linked to a number of high-profile celebrities, including Ariana Grande, Kim Kardashian, and Phoebe Dynevor.
  • Despite having a net worth of $8 million himself, the women Davidson dates have net worths ranging from the six figures to over $1 billion.
  • Davidson's relationships often tend to be short-lived and whirlwind romances, with some lasting only a few months or even weeks.

Pete Davidson is one of the most famous comedians today. He got his start on Saturday Night Live! and immediately grew in popularity. Davidson is from Staten Island, New York, and his early life inspired his film The King of Staten Island. Over the years, the comedian has starred in many other films, including Good Mourning and the recent film Meet Cute. He also has several comedy specials on the streaming service Netflix.

Davidson’s celebrity status has also opened the door for him to date many celebrities over the years. Fans are always baffled by the amount of gorgeous celebrity women Davidson has been linked to. He was previously engaged to singer Ariana Grande, but the two broke things off before they could walk down the aisle. Davidson has also dated actresses and reality television stars, including his recent relationship with Kim Kardashian.

Here are Pete Davidson’s ex-girlfriends, ranked by net worth.

Updated November 2023: It is clear based on the net worths of the women that Pete Davidson has dated that their fortune is not what draws him to them. While Davidson has an $8 million net worth of his own, the women that he dates have net worths from the six figures to that of over $1 billion. The most recent of which, Madelyn Cline, who has the least amount of time in the entertainment industry of the women that Davidson has dated, is worth the least at just $800,000. Whether this relationship will stick remains to be seen.

9 Madelyn Cline - Together Since September 2023

Net Worth: $800,000

  • Fortune made from acting career

Madelyn Cline has been in the entertainment industry for just a little over a decade. In that time, she has made her $800,000 net worth from her role in Outer Banks as Sarah Cameron.

Cline and Davidson have only been together since September 2023, but sources say that the two are committed to one another. Cline has been attending Davidson's appearances during the SAG-AFTRA strike, with Outer Banks due for a Season 4 when the strike ends.

8 Phoebe Dynevor - Briefly Dated From March To August 2021

Net Worth: $3 Million

  • Fortune made from acting career

Pete Davidson’s relationship with actress Phoebe Dynevor was basically a fling. Dynevor is known for her role in the hit Netflix original show Bridgerton and has a net worth of $2 million.

Davidson and Dynevor started dating in March 2021. They were never very serious and were really just spending time together. The comedian and actress chose to keep things so casual because they were doing long distance—Davidson lives in New York City while Dynevor lives in England. They stopped seeing each other before August of that year.

7 Margaret Qualley - Dated Summer Of 2019

Net Worth: $3 Million

  • Fortune made from acting career

Pete Davidson certainly trends towards short, whirlwind romances. Following his much-discussed relationship with Kate Beckinsale, he dated another actress for a short time. In the summer of 2019, the comedian was linked to Margaret Qualley.

From her work in the entertainment industry, Qualley has a net worth of $3 million.

The two were spotted several times in Venice, Italy. Qualley was in Europe to promote her film Seberg, and Davidson joined in on the adventure. Their summer abroad, however, was just that—a summer. The two separated early in the fall of 2019.

6 Kaia Gerber - Dated October 2019 To January 2020

Net Worth: $5 Million

  • Fortune built from modeling career

Kaia Gerber is an American model and actress who was born into the entertainment industry. She is the daughter of Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber, and has a net worth of $5 million. She started dating comedian Pete Davidson in October 2019.

The two were spotted on a romantic getaway to Miami. Davidson loves stealing away his girlfriends for sweet vacations. Though he was kind to Gerber, her parents were not fans of their relationship. Gerber’s parents reportedly hoped the relationship would “fizzle out,” which it did by January 2020.

Quickly afterward, Davidson checked himself into rehab to help with his addiction and mental health.

5 Emily Ratajkowski - Dated For 2 Months In November 2022

Net Worth: $8 Million

  • Fortune made from modeling and film career

Fans were shocked in early November of this year when it was announced that Pete Davidson was seeing actress and model Emily Ratajkowski. Ratajkowski has a net worth of $8 million from her work in the entertainment industry.

Davidson’s relationship with Ratajkowski started shortly after he and Kardashian broke up. Davidson, however, was not the only one on the rebound. Ratajkowski had recently gotten a divorce from Sebastian Bear-McClard after discovering he had cheated on her multiple times. The former couple now co-parent their son Sylvester.

“Pete and Emily have been talking for a couple months now,” a source revealed to Us Weekly, shortly after they started dating. Their relationship is "in the very early stages, but both really like each other." Unfortunately, the romance was short-lived and the two parted ways two months later.

4 Cazzie David - Dated May 2016 To May 2018

Net Worth: $10 Million

  • Fortune made from acting and writing career

Pete Davidson’s first long-term relationship—and his longest relationship to date—was with Cazzie David. They met through the world of comedians since she is the daughter of comedian Larry David. The two of them dated for two years starting in May 2016 and ending in May 2018.

David wrote about their breakup and mental health struggles in her book No One Asked For This. According to her book, Davidson broke up with David the day before his relationship with singer Ariana Grande went public. Needless to say, David was not thrilled by the news of his new relationship and potential crossover.

David reportedly has a net worth of $10 million.

3 Kate Beckinsale - Dated January 2019 To April 2019

Net Worth: $25 Million

  • Fortune made from film career

When Pete Davidson started dating actress Kate Beckinsale in January 2019, fans viewed the relationship as a rebound from his engagement to Ariana Grande. They were first linked at a Golden Globes after-party, and packed in the PDA at every outing. Their twenty-year age gap also made people turn heads.

Not surprisingly, Davidson’s relationship with the Much Ado About Nothing actress did not last long. By April 2019, the two were already over and Davidson had his sights set elsewhere.

From her long and impressive career in acting, Beckinsale has a net worth of $25 million.

2 Ariana Grande - May 2018 To October 2018

Net Worth: $240 Million

  • Fortune made from six albums and TV/film career

Pete Davidson’s relationship with the famous singer Ariana Grande really put him on the map. He was famous in his right before dating Grande, but his name became much more well-known due to the relationship. They began dating in May 2018, and after just a few short weeks, the new couple was engaged. Talk about a whirlwind romance.

Unfortunately for them, their relationship was not built to last. Davidson and Grande broke up in October 2018. The comedian had a tattoo on his neck about her, which he got covered.

Grande has a net worth of $240 million.

1 Kim Kardashian - Dated For 9 Months In 2021

Net Worth: $1.7 Billion

  • Fortune made from reality TV, KKW Beauty, and SKKN

Fans were shocked when it was revealed that Pete Davidson was dating Kim Kardashian. The two met when Kardashian hosted Saturday Night Live and began dating in late 2021.

Davidson was the first person Kardashian publicly saw romantically after her complicated divorce from rapper Kanye West.

Kardashian, from her reality show, clothing and skincare line, and more, has a net worth of $1.7 billion.
