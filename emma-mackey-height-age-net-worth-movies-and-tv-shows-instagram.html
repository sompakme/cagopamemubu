Emma Mackey Height, Age, Net Worth, Movies And Tv Shows, Instagram

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Emma Mackey is a French-British actress best recognized for her excellent performance on the Netflix comedy-drama series Sex Education.

Emma Margaret Marie Tachard-Mackey was born on January 4, 1996, in Le Mans, France as the daughter of a French father and an English mother. Her father works as a school headmaster.

Emma Mackey was raised in Sablé-sur-Sarthe. She received her baccalauréat in 2013 with High Honours at l’académie de Nantes. Emma later studied English language and literature at the University of Leeds and graduated in 2016 after relocating to England.

Emma Mackey is a dual French-British citizen. She rose to prominence on the big screen following her excellent portrayal of the role of Maeve Wiley on the Netflix comedy-drama series Sex Education. The act earned her a BAFTA Television Award nomination for Best Female Comedy Performance in 2021.

Emma Mackey also made appearances in Kenneth Branagh’s 2022 mystery thriller film Death on the Nile (the sequel to 2017’s Murder on the Orient Express) as Jacqueline de Bellefort. She was cast in the LuckyChap Entertainment film Barbie in March 2022.
