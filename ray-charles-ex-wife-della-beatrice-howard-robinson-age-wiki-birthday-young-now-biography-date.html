Della Beatrice Howard Robinson is the ex-wife of famous R&B musician Ray Charles who passed away in 2004. Robinson met Charles in 1954, and they both married a year later on April 5, 1955. Although Charles’s second wife, their marriage lasted 22 years.

Robinson had three sons with Charles: Ray Charles Robinson Jr., David Robinson, and Robert Robinson.
