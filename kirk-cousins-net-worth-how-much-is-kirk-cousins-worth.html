Kirk Cousins’ Contract Details and Salary

Kirk Cousins, the talented NFL quarterback, has a lucrative contract with the Minnesota Vikings. Let’s take a closer look at the details of his contract and salary:

In March 2022, Cousins signed a one-year contract extension worth $35 million. This deal ensured his continued presence with the Vikings for the upcoming season.

However, in March 2023, the team restructured his contract to create more salary cap space. The restructuring involved converting Cousins’ $20 million roster bonus into a signing bonus and adding two additional void years to his contract. This move allowed the team to comply with the salary cap regulations and pushed $16 million of his payment to the void years.

As of 2023, Cousins’ base salary is $10 million. Additionally, he will receive a restructuring bonus of $20 million, bringing his total salary for the year to $30 million.

Overall, Kirk Cousins’ contract details and salary demonstrate the financial commitment of the Minnesota Vikings and the value placed on his talents as their quarterback.
