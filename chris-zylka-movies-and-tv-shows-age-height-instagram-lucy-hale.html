Chris Zylka Movies and TV Shows, Age, Height, Instagram, Lucy Hale

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Chris Zylka, the American actor and model, has carved a successful path in the entertainment industry, earning recognition for his diverse roles in both film and television. With a growing fan base and a notable career, Chris Zylka’s journey is an intriguing one. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at his filmography, age, height, Instagram presence, and past relationship with actress Lucy Hale.

A Versatile Actor’s Filmography

Born on May 9, 1985, in Howland Township, Ohio, Chris Zylka has made his mark through a variety of roles in movies and TV shows. Some of his notable works include:

  • “The Secret Circle” (2011-2012): A supernatural drama series where he showcased his acting prowess.
  • “The Leftovers” (2014-2017): His role as Tom Garvey in this HBO series earned him recognition and critical acclaim.
  • “Kaboom” (2010): Zylka’s presence in this indie film added to his filmography.
  • “Piranha 3DD” (2012): A horror-comedy where he played a memorable role.
  • “Shark Night” (2011): His performance in this thriller film contributed to his growing popularity.
  • “The Amazing Spider-Man” (2012): A major blockbuster in which he portrayed the character Flash Thompson.
  • “My Super Psycho Sweet 16” (2009): A notable role in this teen slasher film.
  • “Bare” (2015): Adding depth to his portfolio, Zylka’s performance in this drama film was well-received.
  • “My Super Psycho Sweet 16: Part 2” (2010): A sequel where he reprised his role.
  • “Welcome to Willits” (2016): A science fiction horror film showcasing his versatility.

Age and Height

As of 2023, Chris Zylka is 38 years old, with a height of 1.85 meters (approximately 6 feet 1 inch).

Instagram Presence

Past Relationship with Lucy Hale

Chris Zylka’s personal life has also been in the spotlight. He was previously in a relationship with actress Lucy Hale, best known for her role in the ABC Family series “Pretty Little Liars.” Their romance garnered media attention, but their nine-month relationship came to an end.

A Dynamic Career and Personal Journey

Chris Zylka’s journey in the entertainment industry has been marked by a diverse range of roles in film and television. As he continues to evolve as an actor, his career promises more captivating performances and exciting projects. Alongside his professional accomplishments, his personal life, including past relationships like the one with Lucy Hale, has kept fans engaged in his dynamic journey.
