2K Hosting WWE 2K23 Reveal Event On Royal Rumble Weekend

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 21, 2024

The next installment of the WWE 2K video game series could be announced very soon, according to a recent report from Insider Gaming.

The latest report surrounding the state of WWE 2K23 notes that 2K is currently planning on holding an event in San Antonio, Texas on January 28, 2023, the same day as the 2023 Royal Rumble pay-per-view.At the event, the report notes, WWE 2K23 will be revealed, and hands-on gameplay will also be available, as well as details on this year’s 2K showcase, as well.

Insider Gaming’s report also mentions that a new game mode will be unveiled during the event as well. Elsewhere in the report, it’s noted that on February 1, another announcement regarding WWE 2K23 will be made, although it’s not exactly stated what that will be.

Although no official release information is available, the previous game, WWE 2K22, was released in March 2022, and Insider Gaming’s report mentions that WWE 2K23 will follow suit with an as-of-yet undetermined March release.
