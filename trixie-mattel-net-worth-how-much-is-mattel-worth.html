Trixie Mattel’s Career and Achievements

Trixie Mattel’s career has been nothing short of remarkable, marked by numerous accomplishments and successes. After her victory on RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars, she soared to new heights in the entertainment industry, solidifying her position as a multi-talented performer and entrepreneur.

One of the highlights of Trixie Mattel’s career is her success as a singer-songwriter. Her debut studio album, “Two Birds,” released in 2017, reached an impressive #2 on the US Heatseekers chart. She followed it up with another chart-topping album, “One Stone,” in 2018. Mattel’s third album, “Barbara,” released in 2020, further showcased her musical prowess and cemented her status as a rising star in the music industry.

“Trixie Mattel’s music is a delightful blend of catchy melodies and clever lyrics that resonate with audiences worldwide,” said Rolling Stone magazine.

In addition to her music career, Trixie Mattel has also made waves on the screen, appearing in popular TV shows such as American Horror Story: Roanoke and Super Drags. Her versatile acting skills have captivated audiences and further expanded her fanbase.

Furthermore, Trixie Mattel starred in the critically acclaimed documentary film “Trixie Mattel: Moving Parts” in 2019, which provided an intimate look into her life and career. The film received rave reviews and showcased Mattel’s vulnerability and authenticity, solidifying her status as not just a drag queen but a compelling and relatable public figure.
