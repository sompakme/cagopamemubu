Timothee Chalamet And Selena Gomez's Real Relationship Is Downright Mysterious, Here's What We Know

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024

Highlights

  • Timothee Chalamet and Selena Gomez have never confirmed dating rumors despite speculation.
  • They appear to be friends.
  • Selena Gomez mentioned a conversation about someone not wanting to take a picture with her at the Golden Globes, which many thought to have been Chalamet.

While Timothee Chalamet is newer to the Hollywood scene and music superstar Selena Gomez has been famous for a long time, both stars are used to hearing rumors about their lives. In particular, people are always interested in who they are dating. For instance, there's also a lot of focus on Chalamet and Kylie Jenner's romance, which some think could be PR.

As soon as the 2024 Golden Globe Awards aired, fans began talking about Timothee Chalamet and Selena Gomez thanks to a confusing situation that came up. Here's the truth about the relationship between the two stars and what happened during the always entertaining awards ceremony.

Have Timothee Chalamet And Selena Gomez Dated?

According to J-14, some people wondered if Selena Gomez was one of Timothee Chalamet's girlfriends. Although people began sharing photos of them kissing, these pictures were stills of A Rainy Day In New York, the movie that they co-starred in. While there were rumors that the two dated, they have never confirmed that. There is no evidence that this is true.

Chalamet and Gomez seem to be pals, and it's possible that they met on the set of A Rainy Day In New York and maintained a platonic relationship after that.

In 2018, E! News reported that in February 2018, Chalamet and Gomez met for drinks at Hollywood's Sunset Tower along with a few other people. Even if people wondered if they were dating at the time, there is no evidence that they were.

Although Selena Gomez is often photographed hanging out with other celebrities, she isn't necessarily best friends with all of them. It's unclear if she considers Timothee Chalamet a good friend or someone she is more casually connected to.

Was Selena Gomez Talking About Timothee Chalamet During The 2024 Golden Globes?

Whether people watched the 2024 Golden Globes or not, one thing is clear: everyone has been obsessed with whether Selena Gomez talked about Timothee Chalamet during the awards ceremony.

According to The Daily Mail, there was a video suggesting that Selena Gomez, Keleigh Sperry, and Taylor Swift were chatting about the actor. The publication spoke to a forensic lip reader, Jeremy Freeman, who said that he knew what kind of conversation the three stars were having. According to Freeman, Gomez said "He didn't want a picture with me, he said no" and Sperry asked "Timothee?" Freeman added that when Swift responded by whispering to Gomez, Gomez replied "I tried, I couldn't, it'll upset them."

If Timothee Chalamet refused to take a photo with Selena Gomez at the Golden Globes, that would have been a pretty dramatic and juicy story. However, that doesn't appear to be what actually happened. According to People, while fans thought that Kylie Jenner didn't want Selena Gomez to snap a picture with her boyfriend Timothee Chalamet, that wasn't the case at all.

A source commented on the Gomez/Chalamet rumor and said:

"she was absolutely not referencing anything about Timothée or Kylie."

The source added that the singer and actress "never even saw or spoke to them." Since Swift, Gomez, and Sperry are friends, it definitely seems that they were just enjoying themselves and chatting about something else. Unless one of them publicly explains what they were talking about, there is no way to know.

While of course the Golden Globes are an exciting part of each year's awards season, it seems like Gomez's friends were happy to spend time together both during and before the event. People reported that Sperry posted some pictures of her and Swift before the Globes. They were smiling and standing against a wall with flowers on it. Sperry also posted a photo of the stars walking through the event venue arm in arm.
